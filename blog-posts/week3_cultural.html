My Thinking Style

September 11, 2014

Thinking Style

After taking the 'Personal Thinking Styles' quiz, it turns out I tied between Concrete Sequential and Abstract Sequential. I feel this is a pretty close representation of how I tend to learn. I also scored pretty high in Concrete Random. Overall I feel this test got me pretty well.

After looking at some of the ways a CS/AS thinker learns best I feel I can use these techniques to help my team and also help myself to adapt or try different studying techniques. For example at DBC I will have access to experts and I will be in a stimulating environment I will be able to learn really well. Also I will be able to communicate my ideas to my team.

Up to this point in Phase 0, I do notice that some of the struggles a CS/AS thinker has I have had as well. These tend to be focused around dealing with abstract ideas and demands to "use your imagination". I feel I have always had this problem as I tend to reason and be 'too' logical.

Fixed vs. Growth Mindset

Between these two mindsets, I feel I heavily fall in a Growth mindset. I think it has to do with the way I brought and also growing up with a grandma that had a degree in child development must have helped with my mindset.

Action Plan

Knowing my mindset and thinking styles, I am going to start doing tasks that require creativity and researching on how to deal with absract ideas.
