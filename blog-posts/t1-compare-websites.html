Comparing a few favorite websites

August 29, 2014

Today I will be comparing three of my favorite websites in terms of user experience and design. The three sites are: https://www.ableton.com, http://www.bostonglobe.com and http://us.illyissimo.com. Lets begin.

Ableton Ableton is very organized and color. It really grabs you at the start by having a large hero image. I find that it helps engage the user in the content by providing several links to articles and offers great navigation from the home page. On inner pages, there seems to be a script that auto loads more content and prevents the user from scrolling to the bottom of the page right away.

Boston Globe The Boston Globle offers a great experience for a news website. It is responsive, well organized, simple in terms of the color used and is pretty easy to navigate. However due to the color choice it can get a bit boring after being on a site.

illy issimoIlly issimo offers a site that is very bold, advertises its product very well and it is very easy to navigate (It's only two pages). The site also offers a responsive experience thus users can easily learn about their product

What do all these sites have in common? All these sites offer a responsive experience, they are fairly easy to navigate and do a great job designing their site to meet their users needs. For example, Boston Globe is fairly straight forward and is not overwhelmed with colors.
