Redesign of My Website

Written by Ronald Chacon on October 20, 2014.

Rebuilding my website was a great experience. I was able to work with Zurb Foundation to redo the design of the website. I feel Foundation 5 is possibly better than Bootstrap 3. I will eventually be depolying my blog section using Jekyll to make writing my blog posts easier.

Overall I think keeping a blog of the past 9 weeks has been very helpful because I was able to reflect on certain cultural subjects and also research on specific technical subjects.

Another reason I have enjoyed keeping a blog is the ability to reflect on my experience during the Phase 0 portion of DBC. This helped me understand subjects much better and also retain information much easier.
