My thoughts on: Stereotype Threat

Written by Ronald Chacon on September 30, 2014.

I feel I have never been at the affects of Stereotype Threat. Granted from the perspective of someone else, I assume they would I be. For example I am hispanic, and a general stereotype is that we all live under one house house that includes our extended family. While this may can be true, it really depends on the financial situation.

Overall I have not really been affected by stereotypes personally. It is something that I never cared cared about as I am my own person and it really doesn't matter what other people might think. If suspected of someone feeling the effects a stereotype, I would simply talk to them and help them get over it.

At this time I feel just being myself is enough to change my environment. I am not going to waste my time, to change someone else's perspective of my culture. I think that is up to them and having an open mind.

Ronald Chacon
