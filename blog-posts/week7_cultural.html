Values and Me

Written by Ronald Chacon on October 11, 2014.

The values I feel that relate to me most during times of happiness, satisfaction, and proud moments:

  • Confidence

  • Knowledge

  • Effectiveness

These are just a few words, I feel that can describe me the best.

Coolness under fire

I feel this phrase can really describe me well as I always have a cool mind in intense moments or where there is pressure to get something done. I believe this is a very important trait to me as I can keep a level head and still perform.
