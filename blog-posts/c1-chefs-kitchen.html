August 29, 2014

Based on the video, the experience at DBC seems like it is a memorable one. Students from all over the place are there to learn and are in it together instead of just being on your own. DBC to me is a legit place to learn not just Ruby and JS, also to learn how to 'learn' and keep learning after DBC.

I feel I would love this culture as being there with other people are who are passionate about learning is very inspirational and also motivation to keep going. My expectations have not changed about DBC. DBC is going to provide everyone with the tools to succeed and I cannot wait to begin.
