What is Pair Programming

It is a concept of software development. Pair programming is one of the best ways to accelerate problem solving and getting the task done.

What is Pair Programming Important to DBC

Allows us to get a better feel to solve problems, communications, a development standard and also the ability to learn from each other. Also a great method to setup a Mentor/Mentee program.
